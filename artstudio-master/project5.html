BSM Asset Booking App (MARA)

The BSM Asset Booking App (MARA) is a digital application developed to streamline the asset booking process for the Human Resource Department (Bahagian Sumber Manusia) at MARA. The application enables staff to request and reserve organizational assets efficiently through a centralized system.

The system helps manage asset availability, booking records, and usage tracking, reducing manual processes and improving transparency and operational efficiency within the department.
