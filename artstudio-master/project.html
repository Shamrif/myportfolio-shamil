Kindergarten Management System

The Kindergarten Management System is a comprehensive digital platform developed to enhance the efficiency of kindergarten operations. It supports three user roles: Admin, Parent, and Teacher.

Parents can register their children and manage tuition payments, teachers can record attendance and monitor participation, and admins can oversee all registration, payment, and attendance records.
